Neverwinter Circle of Paragons Report

This report will generate a list of attendance qualified Paragons in the Kingdom of Neverwinter. Attendance is considered weekly with the weeks starting on Monday and ending on Sunday. (Corpora 1.1.4) Must have signed-in at any Neverwinter province at least 6 times in the last six months. A maximum of one sign in per week for voting eligibility.

You can press the "Copy to CSV" button to put the results into your clipboard to paste into a spreadsheet.
