Iron Mountains Voting

Individuals: To Check your OWN voting status please use the Can You Vote report as it's less ORK Heavy.

This report will generate a list of players within an Iron Mountains park that can vote (Contributing Member). A player must be 14 years or older (V.B.2.3), have a waiver signed (V.B.1.1), attended 6 different weeks in the last 6 months anywhere in the Iron Mountains (V.B.2.a.1), be dues paid (V.B.2.a.2) and not have joined within the last six months (V.B.2.a.5). This report assumes the week starts on Monday and ends on Sunday.
If a player is Not marked as a valid voter, see the highlighted red areas for why.

Does not mark false for age less than 14 because the ORK does not track age

The whole list is provided here but the CSV export provides the week numbers that a person has attended for verification.

WARNING: This report will take a while and use data, don't run it on your cell phone data plan! WARNING!!
