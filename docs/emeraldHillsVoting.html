Emerald Hills Voting

This report will generate a list of players within an Emerald Hills park that can vote. A player must have attended 6 times in the last 6 months anywhere in Amtgard with no more than 2 credits counting per week. The player must not have joined within the last six months. The attendance week starts on Monday and ends on Sunday.
If a player is Not marked as a valid voter, see the highlighted red areas for why.

The whole list is provided here but the CSV export provides the week numbers that a person has attended for verification.

WARNING: This report will take a while and use data, don't run it on your cell phone data plan! WARNING!!
